About Me

Hey there! I’m Kaylee, a Computer Information Systems student at the University of South Carolina, fueled by a love for coding, creative problem-solving, and the occasional caffeine overdose. I’m focused on mastering algorithm design, software engineering, and networks, all while minoring in Media Arts and Business Info Management—because why not mix tech with a bit of creativity?

When I’m not debugging code, you’ll probably find me running (literally) through new trails, getting lost in a good book, or leveling up in my favorite video games. Oh, and I’ve also managed a team of 50+ people in the fast-paced world of food service—so I know a thing or two about leadership, multitasking, and keeping my cool under pressure.
